About

KESA (KI Elite Sports Academy) is a private institution situated at a secure, serene and conducive learning environment overlooking Keroka town. We are set to improve our community by using every availabale resource and opportunity we have to grow students who get along with and support one another towards a common goal. We make certain that every single student and family feels welcome and understood on equal terms. We aim to produce students that have personal aspirations for the future that they can articulate and believe in and share with others, and think critically about issues of human interest, artistry, craft, legacy, husbandry, agriculture, and more–and then do so. We wants all students ‘on grade level’.
